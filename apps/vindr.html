Heart Rate Zone Training

Train smarter with personalized heart rate zones based on your resting heart rate and age.

Zone 1: Recovery

50-60% of max HR. Light activity for warm-up and active recovery.

Zone 2: Aerobic Base

60-70% of max HR. Build endurance and burn fat efficiently.

Zone 3: Tempo

70-80% of max HR. Improve aerobic capacity and efficiency.

Zone 4: Threshold

80-90% of max HR. Increase lactate threshold and speed.

Zone 5: VO2 Max

90-100% of max HR. Maximum effort for peak performance.
